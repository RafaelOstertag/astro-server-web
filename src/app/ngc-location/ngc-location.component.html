Location

Longitude must be set. Longitude must be greater than -180.0. Longitude must be smaller than 180.0.
Longitude in decimal degrees. Negative number is west, positive east.
Latitude must be set. Latitude must be greater than -90.0. Latitude must be smaller than 90.0.
Latitude in decimal degrees. Negative number is south, positive north.
Date must be set.
Time must be set.
